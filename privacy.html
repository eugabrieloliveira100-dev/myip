Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: December 2024

1. Zero Logs Policy

Our core mission is to provide network information without compromising your privacy. CheckMyIP.today does not collect, store, or log your IP address or any other browsing data on our servers. Your IP is processed in real-time to show you information and is discarded immediately after the session ends.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide accurate geolocation and IP data, we use trusted third-party APIs (such as ipify.org and ipapi.co). These services may see your IP address to process your request. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use standard analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand general traffic patterns (e.g., how many people visit from a specific country). This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. We do not use advertising cookies to track you across the web.

4. Terms of Use

By using this website, you agree to use it for informational purposes only. Automated scraping of our tools is prohibited to ensure service stability for all users. The information is provided "as is" without warranties of accuracy.
